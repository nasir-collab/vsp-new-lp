ABOUT VSP TMT REBAR

VSP TMT Rebars are manufactured using state-of-the-art Thermo-Mechanical Treatment technology, ensuring a perfect balance of strength and ductility. Our FE 550+ grade bars are specifically designed to meet the rigorous demands of modern infrastructure, providing unmatched seismic resistance and corrosion protection.

With a core of tempered martensite and a soft ferrite-pearlite core, our bars offer superior weldability and bonding strength with concrete, making them the preferred choice for engineers and architects across the nation.
